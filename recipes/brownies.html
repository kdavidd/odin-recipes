Easy Brownies

This waffle recipe starts with two whole eggs, whisked until light and fluffy.

All-purpose flour adds gluten, which gives the waffles structure. You can also use whole wheat flour.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Step 2: In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  3. Step 3: Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
